There are many things we can all do to fight climate change in our day to day lives.


Green Energy

Switch to green energy! Every little bit counts! Solar panels, wind energy, and nuclear power are all excellent ways to produce energy that don't harm the environment.

Reduce Waste, Reuse & Recycle what you can

Preventing waste ending up in landfills is less CO2 in our atmosphere and less land dedicated to waste.

Public Transportaion

Maybe completely switching to green energy isn't 100% feasible. In that case you can do your part by trying to limit how much CO2 pollution you put in the air.



Save Water

Use less water and help prevent pollution in our beautiful natural bodies of water.

Limit your CO2 output

Everything from switching to an eco-friendly car, using your car less and opting for green transportation, and using alternate powersources limit your eco-footprint.

Protect the Environment

This is the only planet we've got. Lets do our part by limiting pollution and waste and helping nature flourish by planting trees and caring for the environment.
