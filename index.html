Welcome to the Fox Tree website! We are an organization that is committed to fighting climate change and committed to preserving our precious forests and the wild life that resides within. Currently we focusing our efforts in Ontario Canada but are looking to expand our Horizons and become a global organization that will help protect our planet. Our mission is to have planted 10,000 trees across Ontario by the end of 2017.
